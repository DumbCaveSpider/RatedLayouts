# Rated Layouts

A Geometry Dash mods that introduces rated layouts for levels, allowing players to see the difficulty rating of a level before attempting it.
Players can earn a new stats called "Blueprint Stars" by completing rated layouts classic levels. This stats is purely for cosmetic purposes and use for tracking in the leaderboards.

## Features

- Rated Layouts: Levels can now have a difficulty rating (Easy, Normal, Hard, Harder, Insane, Demon) displayed on their layout.
- Blueprint Stars: Players earn Blueprint Stars for completing rated layouts classic levels. The number of stars earned depends on the difficulty rating of the level.
- Leaderboard Integration: Players can see their Blueprint Stars on the leaderboards, allowing them to track their progress and compare with others.
- Rated Layouts Filter: Players can filter levels by their rated layouts in the level browser.
- Blueprint Difficulty Faces: New difficulty faces for rated layouts levels and has a special Blueprint rings around the difficulty face.
- Layout Moderators: New role for level moderators who can send layout levels for it to get rated. Levels can also track how many sends it has for rating.
- Rating System: Similar to Robtop's current rating system, but for rated layouts levels. Levels can be rated by layout moderators based on their design and difficulty.

### All rated levels must go through the developer of this Geode Mod (ArcticWoof) for it to receieve a rating.
